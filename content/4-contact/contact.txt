#Pricing & FAQ

If you’re considering working with Pine, it’s likely that you have a few questions. We know your time is valuable, so we’ve compiled a list of the most important information. Please let us know if you have any other questions we can answer.

*Can I see your pricing sheet?*
Yes, of course! Because each client’s needs are different, we prefer to learn more about your project in order to create a detailed quote. However, our pricing sheets reflects minimum costs for all of the services we offer. If your needs aren’t included on the list, please inquire. We might be able to help, or help you find a trusted colleague who can. (link: /pine-pricing.pdf text: Click here to download our pricing.)

*How long does your process take?*
We prefer a minimum of three weeks to complete any project. Please keep in mind that printing usually requires an additional two weeks.

*What methods of payment do you accept?*
Payment by check or Square Cash are preferred, however we can also accommodate online payment if necessary.
